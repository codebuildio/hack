Wanna Know Our Mission?

Our mission is to bring real problems into light so that the people who wish to create solutions and help the society can achieve so. Bringing out innovation as a product of the teamworks.

What you will learn?

Event Guidelines

Rules are the backbone of any event's successful completion

The team members need to bring their own laptops, cell phones, mobile charger, power bank etc. Internet connection will be provided at the venue. The organizing committee holds the right to disqualify any team. Any type of misbehavior with the judges will result in disqualification. The organizing committee will not be liable if any team or individual lost his/her laptop, cellular phones, gadgets and other stuffs.

Check List

  • All submissions will remain the intellectual property of the the individuals that developed them.
  • The members of each team undertake not to present projects that have previously been submitted to other initiatives.
  • The hackathon will continue for 12 hours. Each team must stay for 12 hours. The team leader will give a presentation on their proposed solution after the development phase.
